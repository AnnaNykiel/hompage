Curriculum vitae

Fictional character, the main character of the Harry Potter series of fantasy books and films, son of James and Lily Potter. As an idea, Potter was born in the mind of the author, J.K. Rowling, in the first half of the 1990s.

Where and how many times did Harry meet Lord Voldemort?

in Godric's Hollow when he was one year old on Halloween in 1981
in the basement of Hogwarts in 1992
in the Chamber of Secrets in 1993
at Little Hangleton Cemetery in 1995
at the Ministry of Magic in 1996
at the Battle of Little Whinging in July 1997
again in Godric's Hollow in December 1997
in the Forbidden Forest in May 1998
and in the Great Hall of Hogwarts, where it all ended, in May 1998.
